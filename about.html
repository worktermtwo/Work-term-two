About Hospitality Services

Hospitality Describes themselves as follows: Hospitality Services is a self-supporting, break-even Department of the University of Guelph that manages
numerous dining facilities and retail shops on campus. I feel that this does not do the department service however. In regards to technology alone,
they support dozens of retailers on campus with infrastructure to make instant tap payments through chip technology on Student Cards, Manage the
meal plans of thousands of students and employees, Manage connections with off campus retailers to allow UoG meal plans to pay for goods and
services, and so much more. Hospitality Services is one of the largest food and retail operations among University campuses. It hosts a very complex IT
infrastructure and supports a clientele of over 25,000 students and faculty with an annual budget of 35M. Technologically it spans a very wide IT
infrastructure with multiple vendor affiliations and partnerships.

IT Services at Hospitality is composed of only 4 full time employees, which is incredibly impressive given the scale of their work.
